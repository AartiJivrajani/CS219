Speaking of amazing places to visit in Bali, here are a couple places I wouldn't mind going back to again and again....

Place Description More info
Monkey Forest Go here if you want to surrounded by monkeys, and also have an opportunity to play with them ;) (yes, I love playing with monkeys, they are actually friendly, well most of them are) Official Website
Tanah Lot Tanah Lot is a temple sitting atop a large rock formation. Over the years, the high tide has separated the rock from the mainland. A must visit if you want to just close your eyes and listen to sea waves crashing on the rocks along with a mellow chime of the temple bells More info
Scuba Diving I would be remiss if I didn't recommend going scuba diving in Bali. If someone were to ask me to recall my "zen" moment, I would ALWAYS talk about the time I went scuba diving in the waters of Bali. The only sound you can hear is that of you breathing and the air bubbles coming out of your scuba mask. They say silence can be deafening, but believe me, this silence was pure bliss! More info
